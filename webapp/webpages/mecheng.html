Green Clean - Soap Bar Grater | Spring 2020

Product Management
Design Thinking
Iterative Design
Design for Manufacturing
SolidWorks

Summary:

Designed a startup product called Green Clean, a bar soap shaving dispenser. With 73 million single use disposed soap bars costing the hotel industry upwards of $30 million in the US, our product aims to reduce wastage, save costs, and allow hotels to switch to a more environment friendly business model. As Team Lead, I utilized product management principles to ensure the timeliness and quality of the product. I also implemented design thinking principles throughout the product development process and conducted economic analysis to determine the market scope and value proposition for the business model of the product.

Heading Machine | Spring 2020

Design for Manufacturing
SolidWorks
3D Design

Summary:

Designed and tested a prototype billet Heading Machine with continuous billet feeding capabilities using Solidworks simulations. The Heading Machine was designed to use plasticine at room-temperature as a substitute for steel at high temperature. Continuous billet feeding allows the Heading Machine to be semi-automated, leading to higher production volumes and significant labor cost savings. I was responsible for the overall design of the machine and the 3D CAD representation of it. I designed the machine keeping in mind Design for Manufacturing principles.

Project Partners: Boris Fedorov, Harald T. Rieber, Henry Ly
